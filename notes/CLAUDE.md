# Notes

Freeform notes, thoughts, and observations that don't fit neatly elsewhere.

## What belongs here

- Meeting notes, daily logs, shower thoughts
- Quick captures and ideas to revisit later
- Drafts and rough thinking

## Organization

- Prefix with date when chronological order matters: `2026-03-14-topic.md`
- Otherwise use a descriptive filename: `idea-for-caching.md`
- Add a `# Title` as the first line of every note.
