# Me

Personal information — who you are, what you care about, and where you're headed.

This is a special entity folder for the repository owner's own profile.

## What belongs here

- Bio, background, and professional identity
- Goals (short-term and long-term)
- Preferences, values, and principles
- Self-reflections and retrospectives

## Organization

- Use thematic filenames: `goals.md`, `values.md`, `bio.md`
- Update files in place rather than creating new versions.
